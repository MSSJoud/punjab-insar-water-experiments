In the original synthetic setup, the median RMS elastic displacement was only $1.77\\times10^{-5}$ mm, whereas the median RMS poroelastic displacement was $1.158$ mm, giving a median poroelastic-to-elastic ratio of $7.35\\times10^{4}$. The median total RMS displacement was also $1.158$ mm, effectively identical to the poroelastic term, showing that the forward problem was overwhelmingly dominated by the poroelastic contribution and motivating the balanced synthetic benchmark used in the rest of the study.
